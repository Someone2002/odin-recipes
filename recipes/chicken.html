Marry Me Chicken

Home

A one-pot recipe, this Marry Me Chicken Pasta has perfectly cooked chicken breast, sun-dried tomatoes, and a creamy sauce all tossed together with pasta. It's done in 30 minutes and the perfect date night dinner recipe. It's so good it may even end up in a marriage proposal!

  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • Sundried Tomatoes
  • Pasta
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Spices and Seasonings
  1. In a small bowl mix together the salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika. Season the chicken with this mixture and set aside
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and prepare the pasta al dente according to package directions.
  3. While the pasta cooks, add the olive oil to a large skillet over medium high heat. Cook the chicken on one side for 5 minutes until it's golden brown.
  4. Flip the chicken over and continue cooking for an additional 5-7 minutes or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  5. Add the butter to the pan along with the sliced shallot and cook for 1-2 minutes. Be sure to scrape up the brown bits of chicken left in the bottom of the pan to get it in the delicious sauce!
  6. Sprinkle the all purpose flour in and stir to coat the shallots in the flour. 1 cup at a time add the chicken stock in, stirring in between for 2-3 minutes to allow the sauce to thicken up.
  7. Stir in the sundried tomatoes and Italian seasoning.
  8. Reduce to medium heat and then stir in the heavy cream and Parmesan cheese and stir until the cheese is melted.
  9. Add pasta to the dish and toss to combine.
  10. Top with the cooked chicken and some fresh basil
